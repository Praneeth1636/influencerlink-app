# Terrace Design Notes

## Visual Direction

Terrace should feel like a quiet professional social app: Instagram-familiar for creator discovery, Figma-clean for product surfaces, and lightly Apple-esque in spacing and restraint.

## Palette

- Warm ink: `#37352f`
- Soft text: `#787774`
- Orange accent: `#D86B3D`
- Pale orange surface: `#fff3ec`
- Light blue accent: `#8CC9E8`
- Pale blue surface: `#eaf7fd`
- Border: `#e9e9e7`
- App surface: `#fbfbfc`

## UI Rules

- Keep authenticated pages airy. Prefer fewer navigation items over packed sidebars.
- Mobile font sizes should be smaller than desktop, not the same layout squeezed down.
- Use icons sparingly and consistently. Icons support labels; they are not the brand.
- Creator-facing surfaces should feel social and visual.
- Brand-facing surfaces should feel searchable and operational.
- Motion should be subtle: hover, selection, loading, and reveal only.
