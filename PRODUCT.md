# Terrace Product Contract

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

Terrace is a creator-brand network where creators publish proof, discover gigs, rank in niches, and connect with brands. Brands search creators with audience and growth filters, post gigs, review applicants, and message creators.

## MVP Journey

1. A new user signs up with Google, Apple, or email.
2. After auth, they land on onboarding and choose Creator or Brand.
3. Creator onboarding collects handle, profile photo, cover photo, bio, niche, location, and social links. Social links become visible on the creator profile. Platform analytics can be connected later or shown from demo data for MVP.
4. Creator activation can prompt them to follow creators. They can skip.
5. Creator home is Feed: posts from followed creators and connected platforms, with platform source, media, likes, shares, comments, and optional analytics visibility.
6. Creator Gigs shows companies looking for influencer promotion. Creators apply when they fit.
7. Ranks shows top creators by niche and overall.
8. Search lets creators find, follow, and connect with other creators.
9. Messages supports creator-brand deal conversations.
10. Notifications covers follows, replies, applications, and deal activity.
11. Profile shows social links, followers/following/posts, per-platform metrics, engagement, growth, and AI suggestions when content underperforms.
12. Brand users share Feed, Messages, Notifications, and Profile surfaces, but Search becomes a brand discovery tool with filters for engagement, growth, audience demographics, niche, location, and platform.
13. Brand Gigs lets brands post gigs and review applicants.

## MVP Real vs Demo

- Real: auth, onboarding role choice, creator profile basics, brand profile basics, feed route, gigs route, search route, ranks route, messages route, notifications route.
- Demo acceptable: social platform analytics, AI underperformance analysis, follower suggestions, advanced demographic filters, payment and escrow.
- Not MVP: real payments, contracts, full social API approval, mobile native apps.

## Product Tone

Calm, spacious, Apple/Figma-adjacent, creator-native. Avoid packed dashboards, oversized desktop typography on mobile, generic icon-heavy SaaS UI, and noisy decorative effects.
